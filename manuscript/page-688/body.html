'You are thinking,' he said, 'that my face is old and tired. You are thinking that I talk of power, and yet I am not even able to prevent the decay of my own body. Can you not understand, Winston, that the individual is only a cell? The weariness of the cell is the vigour of the organism.

Do you die when you cut your fingernails?' He turned away from the bed and began strolling up and down again, one hand in his pocket. 'We are the priests of power,' he said. 'God is power. But at present power is only a word so far as you are concerned.

It is time for you to gather some idea of what power means.
