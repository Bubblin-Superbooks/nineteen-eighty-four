She would be hanging about among the stalls, pretending to be in search of shoelaces or sewing-thread. If she judged that the coast was clear she would blow her nose when he approached; otherwise he was to walk past her without recognition.

But with luck, in the middle of the crowd, it would be safe to talk for a quarter of an hour and arrange another meeting. 'And now I must go,' she said as soon as he had mastered his instructions. 'I'm due back at nineteen-thirty.
